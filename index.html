The Vacation Planner lets you build a complete vacation itinerary on your phone.

  • Create unlimited vacations with start & end dates, attach excursions or day‑trips, and schedule reminders.

  • Share any trip easily and export the full schedule to friends & family and set reminders for vacations and excursions.

  • Search for vacations by date range and join your friends.

The first account you create becomes the administrator, giving you full control over additional users.
