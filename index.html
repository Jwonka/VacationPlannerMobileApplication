Vacation Planner lets you build a complete vacation itinerary on your phone. Create unlimited vacations with start & end dates, attach excursions or day‑trips, and schedule reminders.

Share any trip easily and export the full schedule to friends & family or set reminders for vacations and excursions. Have free time? Search for vacations by date range and join your friends.

The first account you create becomes the administrator, giving you full control over additional users and data backups.
